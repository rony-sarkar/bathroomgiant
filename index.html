500mm Floorstanding bathroom cloakroom cabinet vanity unit with basin sink White

This modern vanity unit from the brand CUAWI is perfect for any bathroom or cloakroom. The unit is 80 cm in height and features a single tap hole for easy installation. With a sleek and stylish design, this vanity unit adds a touch of elegance to any bathroom. It also features a cupboard for added storage space and comes with all the necessary assembly instructions. Made in China, this high-quality unit is perfect for those looking for a functional and attractive addition to their bathroom.
